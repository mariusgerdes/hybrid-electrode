# Video and Photo Documentation

This directory contains demonstration videos and photos of the electrode system and experimental procedures.

## Available Files

### Videos
- `fillingVideo.mp4` - Demonstration of filling the electrode with conductive gel
- `cleaningVideo.mp4` - Electrode cleaning and maintenance procedure

### Photos
- `electrodeTopAndSide.jpg` - Top and side view of the assembled electrode
- `filledElectrode.jpg` - Electrode filled with conductive gel
- `fillingSyringe.jpg` - Syringe setup for electrode filling
- `filling_utensils.jpg` - Tools and materials needed for filling

## Video Descriptions

### Filling Video (`fillingVideo.mp4`)
Demonstrates the proper technique for filling the RPH-Trode electrode chambers with conductive gel using a syringe. Shows:
- Preparation of filling materials
- Injection technique to avoid air bubbles
- Verification of proper fill level

### Cleaning Video (`cleaningVideo.mp4`)
Shows the maintenance and cleaning procedure for the electrodes after use:
- Disassembly steps
- Cleaning solution recommendations
- Proper drying and storage

## Usage Notes

- Videos are in MP4 format and can be played in any standard media player
- For best viewing, download the files rather than streaming in the browser
- Photos are high-resolution JPG format

## Safety and Best Practices

When preparing electrodes:
1. Use sterile or clean syringes
2. Avoid introducing air bubbles during filling
3. Clean electrodes thoroughly between uses
4. Follow institutional biosafety protocols
